The Midnight Library

Matt Haig

  • Material type: TextText
  • Language: English
  • Publication details: London : Kingfisher, 2000
  • Subject(s): Engineering | Kites
  • Average rating: 0.0 (0 votes)

    • Matt Haig follows Nora Seed, a woman who finds herself in the Midnight Library after attempting suicide. In this mysterious library, every book represents a different life she could have lived had she made different choices. With the help of the librarian, Mrs. Elm, Nora explores these alternate realities, seeking a life where she feels fulfilled and happy. As she navigates through the pages of her life, Nora learns valuable lessons about regret, forgiveness, and the importance of embracing the present moment. "The Midnight Library" is a moving and uplifting tale that explores the power of second chances and the beauty of finding contentment in life's ordinary moments..
